This bottles were opened at Nasty and Steven’s at their rented apartment near Jiaotong University. It was the night of Cantonese food, which Steven had been making since 4 PM today. Steamed fish, beef fried and steamed with pieces fo cucumber and ginger, and the shot fo the show - curry. There were other things, but it’s not about them. We set and talked about Ukraine, Hong Kong and China. Then we played this game “Pass the border” where the rule is to actually figure what is the rule. We played and I was lifting this young Merlot. It flowed so smoothly and indeed well paired with the meats on the table. When the Reservado was carefully reserved in our bellies we moved to the heartwarming talks sipping Fireball.
